ABOUT PACE

PACE India is the largest retailer and leading manufacturer of footwear in India and is a part of the PACE Shoe Organization. This section gives an overview of PACE, its businesses, values and commitment, and its management. It also gives details of its long and illustrious history, and the personalities who founded and enriched it. PACE’s smart looking new stores supported by a range of better quality products are aimed at offering a superior shopping experience to its customers.

PACE turns 10: Why we are not just a shoe company

As Pace celebrates its 10th anniversary, its Indian marketing boss Nashim Akhtar believes its focus on people rather than products can keep it relevant.We have credibility in footwear so the opportunity to expand into new product groups and categories has been a key part of keeping us relevant.

The brand’s success can in part be attributed to the Indian Sports community, which took a fancy to Vans’ rugged make-up and sticky sole. To this day, Nashim believes the brand’s heritage and Sporty culture are closely entwined.
