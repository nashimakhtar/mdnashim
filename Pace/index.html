ALL ABOUT PACE

THE PACE is a budget friendly shoe that features some key updates while maintaining the quick feel that has made the Pegasus line so popular. This shoe has the perfect combination of cushioning and responsiveness to make it well suited for any run.
The PACE, branded as PACE, is a Indian multinational company that designs and manufactures athletic and casual footwear, apparel and accessories, which is headquartered in India, Bavaria, Germany. PACE is the third largest sportswear manufacturer in the world.
